How to install Minecraft PE Skins?


In this tutorial We will show you how to download, install and setup your skins for Minecraft Pocket Edition on your Android device.

1. Step 1

Choose your Skins/Packs item and tap on the Get button to download item


2. Step 2

Click the OPEN button. Minecraft will launch automatically with a confirmation message on top. Wait to the process complete successful like this:


3. Step 3

Click the Play button to enter the game. Now you can enter the Game settings by choosing Create New World or by editing an existing world. In the next step, We chosen edit the world.

4. Step 4

Click the Profile button to start edit your skin.

Create new Character or Edit Character to change your skin. In there, We choose Edit.

Tap on Classic Skin Tab and Click on OWNER option to expand the list.

Scroll down and choose your skins.


And then. Save your change. Let's to play now.
