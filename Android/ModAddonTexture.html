How to install Minecraft PE Mods/Addons/Texture Packs for Android?


Mods/Addon/Texture have been introduced as an official feature of the Minecraft game and It's only support from version 0.18.0 on Android. Currently,Minecraft is being support Minecraft PE add-ons only for the best experiences. We do not support for ModPE scripts or BlockLauncher addons.

In this tutorial We will show you how to download and install mods/addons/textures for Minecraft Pocket Edition on your Android device.


1. Step 1

Choose your Mod/Addon/Texture Packs item and tap on the Get button to download item


2. Step 2

Click the OPEN button. Minecraft will launch automatically with a confirmation message on top. Wait to the process complete successful like this:


3. Step 3

Click the Play button to enter the game. Now you can enter the Game settings by choosing Create New World or by editing an existing world. In the next step, We chosen edit the world.


4. Step 4

Scroll down the left pane, and tap on the Resource Pack or Behavior Pack menu option. Find your packs and select to Activate on the right pane.
