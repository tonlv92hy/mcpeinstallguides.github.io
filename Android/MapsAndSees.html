How to install Minecraft PE Maps/Seeds?


In this tutorial We will show you how to download and install Maps/Seeds for Minecraft Pocket Edition on your Android device.

1. Step 1

Choose your Maps/Seeds item and tap on the Get Button to Download item


2. Step 2

Click the OPEN button. Minecraft will launch automatically with a confirmation message on top. Wait to the process complete successful like this:


3. Step 3

Click the Play button to enter the game. Now you can enter the Game settings by choosing Create New World or by editing an existing world. In the next step, We chosen edit the world.
